FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Do I need a tax clearance to start trading?

Yes, the law requires that every company be tax complaint and should acquire a tax clearance within 30 days of start of trade. Also other companies will not do business with a company that does not have a tax clearance. Or they will charge you 10% of withholding tax. Also you cannot open your company’s bank account without a tax clearance. Check out our prices for packages with a tax clearance certificate.

Which type of company?
PBC or a PLC?

A PBC (Private Business Corporation) is meant mainly for small operations. Also It is good for those who have a limited budget or specifically want something small. But a PLC (Private Limited Company) accommodates all sizes of businesses from small to large enterprises.

In a PBC the shareholders are also the directors. But If you want the shareholders to be different, from the directors then you should choose a PLC. A PBC is also ideal if you want to be a sole trader. But some types of businesses cannot register as a PBC e.g. a pharmacy or a micro-finance company since the requirements of the MCAZ or the RBZ don’t accommodate a PBC. If your budget permits, choose a PLC as it is more popular and therefore more recognized by almost everyone.

What do I need to register my company?

  • 1 to 5 proposed company names for your Private Limited company.
  • The type of business your company will be focused on.
  • The full names, ID numbers and addresses of the shareholders or owners of the Private Limited Company.
  • The full names, ID numbers and addresses of the directors (between 2 to 50 directors are needed).
  • *The owners/shareholders can be the same as the directors but they can also be different.
  • The issued shared capital of the company.
  • The number of shares issued to each shareholder.
  • Physical and postal address of the business.
  • The signature of each shareholder of the Private Limited Company is needed on the Memorandum & Articles of Association, and 1 Director or company secretary must sign the CR6 and CR14.
  • At least one Zimbabwean as part of the company directors.
